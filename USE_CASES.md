# Use Cases

## Fleet Utilization

Operations managers compare rentable fleet supply to active demand by branch and vehicle class to reposition vehicles and reduce idle inventory.

## Pricing Effectiveness

Revenue managers evaluate whether a rate change improved conversion or simply shifted bookings without increasing realized revenue.

## Booking Pace Monitoring

Branch leadership tracks same-day and near-term pickup demand using near-real-time booking events to detect emerging shortages.

## Maintenance Impact

Fleet teams quantify downtime and revenue-at-risk due to repairs, inspections, and operational holds.

## Demand Forecasting

Planning teams use historical bookings, pricing, and branch capacity features to forecast demand by location and class.
