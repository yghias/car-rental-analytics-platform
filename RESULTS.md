# Results

## Business Outcomes

The platform is designed to deliver measurable improvements across operations, pricing, and reporting.

### Improved fleet utilization visibility
- Branch and vehicle-class utilization can be monitored at daily and intra-day levels from a common metric layer.
- Out-of-service inventory and maintenance downtime are exposed alongside rentable supply, making hidden capacity loss visible.

### Better pricing insights
- Pricing events can be compared to booking pace and realized revenue using a consistent analytical grain.
- Revenue teams gain branch- and class-level visibility into override behavior, discount usage, and conversion trends.

### Faster booking analytics
- Booking lifecycle data moves from source systems into staging and reporting models on a near-real-time to hourly cadence.
- Operational teams no longer rely on manual export reconciliation to understand pickup demand and cancellations.

### More reliable operational reporting
- Quality checks, run metadata, and authoritative-source rules reduce metric drift across teams.
- Executive and finance reporting can be refreshed with reproducible warehouse models instead of ad hoc spreadsheets.

## Illustrative Metrics

- 30 minute booking data freshness target for operational reporting
- 15 minute fleet-status visibility target for streaming-covered events
- 90 percent reduction in manual branch utilization reconciliation effort
- multi-domain reporting foundation for utilization, revenue, downtime, and forecast accuracy in one platform
